Filipino-American Diaspora, Identity, and Music

Given their connections to colonialism and subsequently complex identities, how do young Filipino-Americans participate in music culture in a way that potentially resists the historical oppression from their colonizers and heals historical trauma?

  1. Do today’s young Filipino-Americans experience and emotionally internalize their colonial identities? If so, how?
  2. Does historical trauma operate among generations of people in the Filipino-American diasporic context? If so, how?
  3. Do Filipino-American music artists experience the simultaneous processes of decolonization and healing? If so, how do these people facilitate these processes?
  4. Is contemporary music an effective healing tool among Filipino-Americans? If so, what aspects or qualities of can make it effective? How can this knowledge be applied to restoring Filipino-Americans to a state of “pre-colonial” health?
